About Us

Botshabelo Digital Hub is an incubation hub dedicated to fostering innovative ICT solutions. Established through the collaborative efforts of the Department of Small Business Development (DSBD) and various strategic partners, our mission is to provide businesses with the infrastructure, mentorship, and networking opportunities they need to succeed in today's competitive digital economy.


Since our inception in 2019, we have been committed to nurturing the growth of ICT entrepreneurs by offering a suite of tailored services. Our vision is to create globally competitive enterprises that are equipped to harness the power of technology and innovation.


We believe in an entrepreneurial spirit, collaboration, and a relentless pursuit of excellence, making us the go-to hub for both start-ups and established businesses looking to innovate.
